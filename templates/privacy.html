Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the service, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Specifically:

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To display your expenses and spending summaries back to you.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services to support operations such as hosting and analytics. These services are chosen carefully and are contractually required to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws. We do not allow third parties to use your data for their own purposes.

We use Google Fonts to load typefaces. When your browser loads these fonts, your IP address is sent to Google's servers. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle this data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also object to certain uses of your data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
